Hello

Welcome to our universe! We are an educational studio that makes alternative unlocking systems for your child’s android based devices.

We believe that children should have fun on their devices and what is a greater way to make that play time more rewarding... warming up their brainpower with a few educational problems.

Parents get to set level of difficulty and number of problems to solve according to their child’s level. Once all problems are completed correctly, the child gains access to the rest of their device.
